Question: Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency.

Making sure your project is equipped with the right tools and you know how to use them effectively, if your working solo research or train your self to understand your tools better. Create a library of code and content so you can save time implementing them to your project. Planning your day and having regular breaks from your work will improve all aspects because you are not tired and feel overwhelmed.

Taking the time to learn new Tec and keeping up with the industry will improve all aspects of your product, you will save time in the long run, this will increase your output and quality of work. Making sure your planning is a schedule and not done all at once it is best to break it down in regular standups or meetings this will also improve your productivity.
