Question: Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites.

When building a web site for your self or as a project for a client, all content that is not your creation will have copyright, its best to assume the content you want to use is protected so you will need authorisation and state it on your site, example an image with the original creator or links to the original author. Not meeting the regulations this will be damaging to your reputation and financial costs. If the content is yours is common to state it on your site so it protects you from other sources using your work.
