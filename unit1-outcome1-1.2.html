Question: Describing methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully â€“ what do you need to make projects successful? What tools? What skills?

To make projects successful communication, teamwork and good organization skills are main the strengths of most business. In this modern age, there are a range of tools for digital projects for small or large business, for communication between your self and other team members you could use Slack, Discord, Google Hangouts and video meetings you could use Zoom, Microsoft Teams and Hangouts.

Also for file/project sharing, there is a range of tools but it all depends on your business type. For example, if was a digital designer I would use Dropbox, Google Drive and Apple iCloud. Which can be stored safely on internal hardware or in the cloud for remote access.

Project tools for visual management like Trello or Monday are very helpful to organize your business. Skills are required in all types of projects but all depends on the type and style of business, creative visual would need skills in creative software like Adobe or 3d modelling software. A business will employ someone with a certain skill set but not always essential because team members can train their skills to fit the project requirements.
