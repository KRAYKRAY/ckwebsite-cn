Question: Describing access issues that need taking into account - describe issues that people with disabilities may face when using webpages and how we can overcome them.

A person with disabilities could have access issues with limitation to hand motor skills, sight hindrances, difficulty hearing and neurological disorders, there is a range of disabilities. With the increase of Technolgy, we have tools to help people with disabilities example text size, subtitles for media, page layout, choosing the right colours and even making your website user friendly with touch screen services.
